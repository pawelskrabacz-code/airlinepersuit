AIRLINE
PURSUIT

A 1v1 global aviation deduction game
Online mode · Each player on their own device

How to Play

Online mode · two devices required

Roles

The Hider secretly picks any airport on their device. The Seeker must find it. Each player opens this game on their own device.

Starting

The Hider picks their airport and receives a 6-digit room code. The Seeker enters that code to join. The Hider must not show their screen to the Seeker.

Movement

The Seeker starts at a random hub airport. They can only fly to airports with direct scheduled routes from their current position. Tap any airport to inspect its connections.

Action Points

Seeker starts with 20 AP. Run out and the Hider wins.

  • 1 AP Ask a Yes/No question — sent to Hider in real time
  • 1 AP Fly to a directly connected airport
  • Free Am I Here? — guess your current airport is the target · wrong = −5 AP

Map Controls

Tap your current airport (blue) → opens Fly menu. Tap any other airport → inspect its connections. Double-tap → mark as suspected (amber) or eliminated (faded). Pinch/scroll to zoom.
